Shipping Policy

This page outlines how AvelixLink approaches processing, shipping, tracking, and delivery timing for both retail and wholesale orders.

1. Retail Orders

Processing and Delivery Estimates

Retail orders are normally processed within the timeframe shown or communicated at checkout. Delivery timing depends on destination, selected shipping method, carrier performance, and order verification.

Shipping Cost

Shipping cost is shown or confirmed before payment is completed, depending on the order flow and destination.

Tracking

Tracking information may be provided when available through the selected carrier or fulfillment method.

2. Wholesale Orders

Lead Time

Wholesale lead time is an estimate only. Actual production and dispatch timing may vary based on order size, product complexity, raw-material availability, supplier schedule, inspection requirements, and logistics conditions.

Production Start

Production, sourcing, or allocation for wholesale orders may begin only after required deposit and order confirmation are received.

Shipping Quotation

Wholesale shipping quotation depends on quantity, destination, total weight or volume, packaging method, and selected transport method such as express, air, sea, rail, or consolidated shipping.

3. Duties, VAT, and Import Fees

Customs duties, VAT, import taxes, clearance fees, and related destination charges may be payable by the buyer unless otherwise agreed in writing as part of the confirmed order arrangement.

4. Delays

AvelixLink does not promise guaranteed delivery dates. Delays may occur because of customs review, carrier congestion, weather, local restrictions, force majeure events, port issues, supplier delays, or incomplete shipping documentation.

5. Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for providing complete and accurate shipping information. Additional costs, storage charges, re-delivery fees, return fees, or delivery failure caused by incorrect shipping data may be the customer's responsibility.

6. Contact
